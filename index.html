Our History

Founded in the early 20th century by the visionary entrepreneur, Thomas Evergreen, The Apostle Resort began as a secluded sanctuary for weary travelers. With a passion for hospitality and a commitment to preserving the surrounding natural beauty, Evergreen transformed the rugged coastline into a haven of luxury and elegance. Over the decades, The Apostle Resort has evolved into a premier destination, attracting guests from around the globe with its unparalleled beauty and world-class amenities. From Hollywood celebrities seeking seclusion to adventurous explorers craving outdoor escapades, our resort has welcomed visitors of all backgrounds, each contributing to the rich tapestry of our storied history.
